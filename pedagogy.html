Teaching Philosophy 教学理念

Samuel Oehler-Huang Economics teacher, Suzhou Foreign Language School 苏州外国语学校经济学教师

These IGCSE Economics lessons are designed so students do more than remember definitions. They learn to explain mechanisms, test ideas, use evidence and build exam-ready judgement.

这些 IGCSE 经济学课程不只帮助学生记忆定义，更强调解释经济机制、检验观点、使用证据， 并逐步形成适合考试要求的分析和判断能力。

Core pedagogy cycle

From curiosity to exam explanation / 从好奇到考试表达

Each lesson connects real-world context, conceptual understanding, application practice and exam communication.
每节课通过清晰的学习循环，把现实情境、概念理解、应用练习和考试表达连接起来。

1

Hook
情境导入

Start with a policy problem, market puzzle or real example so the lesson has a reason.

从政策问题、市场现象或真实案例出发，让学生知道为什么要学习这个概念。

2

Inquiry
探究讨论

Students discuss before answers appear, building confidence and oracy.

在呈现答案前先讨论，培养学生的表达、自信和参与感。

3

Explicit concepts
明确概念

Definitions, diagrams and comparisons are taught with concise exam wording.

用简洁、符合考试要求的语言讲清定义、图示和对比关系。

4

Application
迁移应用

Students connect concepts to taxation, market failure, budgets and policy choices.

把概念应用到税收、市场失灵、政府预算和政策选择等真实语境中。

5

Retrieval
提取练习

Fill-in blanks and quizzes make students recall, not just reread.

通过填空和测验促进主动回忆，而不是停留在被动阅读。

6

Exam explanation
考试表达

Students practise chains, balance, limitations and judgement.

训练因果链、正反平衡、局限性和判断，形成可得分的考试表达。

Cambridge alignment

Aligned with Cambridge teaching principles / 对齐剑桥教学原则

This course is aligned with Cambridge guidance while remaining adapted to our students, language context and classroom needs.
本课程对齐剑桥教学指导，同时根据学生特点、语言环境和课堂需要进行本地化设计。

Active learning / 主动学习

Students participate through discussion, prediction, simulations and policy choices. The slide deck is a classroom sequence, not a reading script.

学生通过讨论、预测、模拟和政策选择参与学习；课件是课堂学习流程，而不是阅读稿。

Assessment for learning / 促进学习的评价

Hinge questions, in-lesson quizzes and after-class quizzes create feedback for students and for the teacher's next lesson decisions.

关键问题、课堂测验和课后测验为学生提供反馈，也帮助教师判断下一步教学重点。

Curriculum coherence / 课程连贯性

Lessons follow Cambridge IGCSE Economics 0455 syllabus references and use exam-ready definitions, examples and command-word practice.

课程依据 Cambridge IGCSE Economics 0455 考纲，使用适合考试的定义、案例和指令词练习。

Inclusive bilingual access / 双语支持

Chinese support appears where it lowers access barriers, while the final academic target remains clear English economic explanation.

中文支持用于降低理解门槛，但最终目标仍是清晰、准确的英文经济学表达。

Metacognition and self-management / 元认知与自我管理

Handout and quiz modes help students revisit learning independently, identify gaps and practise before formal assessment.

讲义和测验模式帮助学生自主复习、发现薄弱点，并在正式评价前进行练习。

Technology-supported learning / 技术支持学习

The site gives every learner access to slide view, print view and quiz view, so class teaching continues into structured review.

网站为每位学生提供幻灯片、打印讲义和测验入口，让课堂学习延伸为有结构的复习。

For home and school

What this means in practice / 课堂内外的实际意义

For parents

Students can reopen the exact lesson sequence used in class. This matters because effective revision is not simply reading notes: students need to retrieve ideas, explain links and notice where their understanding is incomplete.

  • Slide view recreates the classroom learning path.
  • Handout view supports calmer review and printing.
  • Quiz view gives quick feedback after independent study.

面向家长

学生可以课后重新打开课堂上使用的同一套学习流程。有效复习不是简单看笔记， 而是要主动回忆概念、解释因果链条，并发现自己还不清楚的地方。

  • 幻灯片模式帮助学生重走课堂学习路径。
  • 讲义模式适合安静复习，也便于打印。
  • 测验模式让学生在自主学习后获得即时反馈。

For teachers

Each lesson is built as a teachable sequence: attention is focused first, concepts are introduced in small steps, checks are placed before misconceptions harden, and exam prompts require students to turn knowledge into explanation.

  • Partial reveals manage cognitive load when a chain or comparison is dense.
  • Real-world facts make abstract syllabus points concrete.
  • Exam prompts rehearse analysis, balance, limitation and judgement.

面向教师

每节课都被设计成可教学的序列：先集中注意力，再分步骤建立概念， 在错误理解固定下来之前进行检查，最后用考试题型促使学生把知识转化为解释。

  • 逐步呈现用于降低复杂因果链和对比内容的认知负荷。
  • 真实案例让抽象考纲内容变得具体。
  • 考试练习训练分析、正反平衡、局限性和判断。
